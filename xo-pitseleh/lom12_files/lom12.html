How to Choose a Map




This tells you which areas of land have naturally occurring element levels. You should take these under
consideration when planning where to place your Mailbox. Here's what the Japanese symbols mean:
= Wisp (light blue)
= Shade (black)
= Dryad (green)
= Aura (yellow)
= Gnome (brown)
= Jinn (blue)
= Salamander (red)
= Undine (purple)

This is a good beginner map for activating all quests your first time through the game. You'll also be
able to get all Demi-Human pets with this and have access to most every type of shop available. If
you're not able to place an artifact like shown here just enter an area and leave and try again. If you
really want to do an even better beginner map, check ANelson's "Map/Artifact Placement Guide" at
www.gamefaqs.com.
