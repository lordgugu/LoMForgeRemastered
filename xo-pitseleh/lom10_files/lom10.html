Notes Before Starting:
-You'll probably wonder about the numbbers next to the skills in the "Requiered Ability" column. They signify the skill level you need to have for that ability in order to learn that Special Technique--more or less. (It's actually a little bit more complex than that and there are some numbers haven't included yet) So how do you know what level one of your abilities is currently at? You don't. Usually I just compare. Like, if I have Sparkling Rampage then I know that my Spin is at least at Level 20 because Spin needs to be at least level 20 to learn Sparkling Rampage. Always works for me.
-Attack power is rated from worst to bbest: E < D < C < B < A < S
-I've included the Japanese ST names wwhen different from the U.S. version. I've left some untranslated for now.
-"Grapple" does slightly bettter damage if your hero is equipped with a glove
-There's only one ST that does Magic ddamage: Elazul's "Laser Blade".
